1. Create a GitHub Account:

Visit the GitHub website (https://github.com/).
Click on the "Sign up" button.
Fill in the required information, including a username, email address, and password.
Complete the account creation process.
2. Verify Your Email:

After signing up, GitHub will send a verification email to the address you provided.
Open the email and follow the instructions to verify your email address.
3. Log into Your GitHub Account:

Visit the GitHub website and log in with the credentials you used to create your account.
